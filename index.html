The First Time I Saw You

02

When Life Gave Me A Chance

Days passed, and I kept wanting to meet her, but I never got the chance. Still, life gave me another opportunity during youth camp. That was the moment I finally gathered the courage to talk to her.

03

The No That Didn't End My Love

When I first confessed my feelings, she said no. And honestly, that could have been the end. But something in my heart refused to let her go. Because from the very beginning, I saw her as someone I could build a future with. I saw her as the one I wanted to grow old with.

04

When You Said Yes

And then one day, everything changed. She said yes. That one moment made my life brighter in ways I can't explain. Since then, life has felt warmer, happier, and more meaningful.

05

Our Journey

I still remember the days we texted, met after her classes, walked down the roads together, laughed, talked, and shared moments that felt like pure happiness. Those simple moments became some of the happiest memories of my life. We first met on 13 July 2016 — and today, the journey is still going.

Through Everything

We've had our ups and downs. Life hasn't always been easy. But even in the hardest times, life has remained beautiful with her. And even now, my prayer is the same — that what we have never fades away.
